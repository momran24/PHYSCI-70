Mustafa is a 16-year-old rising junior at Belmont High School in Belmont, MA. Although he was born in Quebec, Canada and lived there for 13 years, he is originally from Egypt thus explaining his trilingual nature (Arabic, French, English). Mustafa plays Rugby and Swimming where he is respectively state and provincial champion. He has a deep passion for Computer Science, Photography, and Engineering. Mustafa always has been fascinated by technology and the advancments it brings to humanity. In college, he plans to study either Computer Science or Mechanical Engineering. Perhaps, both!
